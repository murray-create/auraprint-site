How to read your proof: the solid black line is the trim line — your finished size. The pink dashed edge is the 3 mm bleed, which is printed then trimmed away. Anything important (text, logos) should sit inside the trim line; background colours and images should extend to the bleed edge.
  • Check all spelling, phone numbers and email addresses carefully.
  • Colours on screen are a guide only — printed colour can vary slightly.
  • Once you approve, the job goes to print exactly as shown.
