Frequently asked questions

What size are real estate corflute signs?

Common sizes are 600x900mm and 900x1200mm photo boards, but we print corflute in any custom size on 3mm or 5mm board. Price your exact size instantly on the corflute page.

Do you offer agency accounts or repeat ordering?

Yes. We work with agencies on repeat templated signboards and stationery so every listing goes up fast and on-brand. Talk to us about your monthly volume.

Can you print sold and auction stickers?

Yes, we produce sold stickers, auction date strips and overlays to update existing boards without reprinting the whole sign.

How fast can I get signs for a new listing?

Corflute boards and A-frames typically turn around in a few business days plus delivery. Need print faster? Business cards, flyers and DL cards have genuine same day and next day options.
