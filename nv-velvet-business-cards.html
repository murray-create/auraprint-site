Frequently asked questions

Why do these feel so good?

The NV (velvet) laminate is a micro-textured soft-touch film over thick artboard - it feels like suede and resists fingerprints.

Can I add foil?

Yes - gold, silver and rose metallic foil, supplied as a separate spot layer in your artwork. Ask if you need help setting it up.
