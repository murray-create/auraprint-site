AUSTRALIAN PAPER SIZES

Every standard size in millimetres, and which one your job actually needs.

By Aura Print & Promo · July 2026 · 3 minute read

Australia uses the international ISO A-series. The system is beautifully simple: A0 is one square metre of paper, and every size after it is the previous one folded in half. Same proportions all the way down, which is why an A4 design scales cleanly to an A3 poster.

Common signage sizes

Wide format doesn't follow the A-series - signs are cut to whatever the job needs. That said, some sizes come up constantly:

Vinyl bannersMade to size - any dimensions
A-frame inserts600 × 900 is the workhorse

Which size should you choose?

  • Letterbox drops and handouts: DL or A5 - big enough to say something, cheap enough to print thousands.
  • In-store promotion: A4 and A3 posters do the everyday work; A2 and above for windows and walls.
  • Events: pull up banners for indoors, corflute and vinyl for outdoors.
  • Not sure? Ask us. Quantity, viewing distance and budget usually make the answer obvious.
