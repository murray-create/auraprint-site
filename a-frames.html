Frequently asked questions

Can I replace just the inserts or stickers?

Yes - keep the frame, swap the message. Pick the inserts-only or stickers-only option in the configurator; it is a fraction of the full price.

Which style should I choose?

Signflute frames are the budget all-rounder. Endurosign and Evarite suit real estate. Chalkboards suit cafes, and Monsoon frames are built for wind. Not sure? Ask us for a quick recommendation.

Are they weatherproof?

All frames are made for outdoor footpath use. UV inks resist fading in Queensland sun; bring lightweight styles in overnight in high wind.
