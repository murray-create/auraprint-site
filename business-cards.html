Why print with Aura?

Frequently asked questions

What is NV Velvet?

NV (Neenah Velvet) is a luxurious soft-touch laminate over thick 450gsm artboard. It feels like suede and makes colours pop. Add Spot UV for glossy highlights over the velvet finish.

How fast can I get my cards?

Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days plus delivery. Next-day express is available on most stocks if you order and approve artwork before 10am.

Can you design my card?

Yes. Our design team can create or refresh your card from $99. Ask via the quote form or live chat.

Do I see a proof before printing?

Always. A digital proof lands in your inbox and nothing prints until you approve it.
