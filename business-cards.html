Frequently asked questions

What is NV Velvet?

NV Velvet is a luxurious soft-touch laminate over thick 450gsm artboard. It feels like suede and makes colours pop. Add Spot UV for glossy highlights.

How fast can I get my cards?

Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days plus delivery. Same-day production is available on selected stocks - choose your turnaround above.

Do I see a proof before printing?

Always. A digital proof lands in your inbox and nothing prints until you approve it.
