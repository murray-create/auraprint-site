Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between kiss cut and die cut?

Kiss cut leaves the backing intact (easy peeling on rolls); die cut cuts through everything for individual stickers. Need die cut? Hit Get a Quote.

Which stock should I choose?

Paper stocks suit indoor labels and packaging. Vinyl is waterproof and scratch-resistant for outdoor or fridge/freezer use. Clear lets your product show through.

Can you apply these by machine?

Yes - these are supplied on rolls wound for machine application. Tell us your applicator direction in the order notes.
